Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Yobou Market ("Yobou", "we", "us") is a multi-vendor marketplace operated as a demo/research app. This policy describes what data we collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to the practices below.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, email, password (bcrypt-hashed, never stored in plain text).
  • Vendor data: business name, phone, optional license URL, approval status.
  • Order data: items purchased, prices at time of purchase, shipping address, order status, simulated payment transaction IDs.
  • Device data: anonymous usage telemetry collected by the underlying platform (Capacitor / Android) — no advertising IDs.

2. How we use it

Data is used solely to operate the marketplace: authenticate users, process orders, advance shipments, display purchase history, and prevent abuse. We do not sell personal data and do not run advertising.

3. Payments

Payments in this app are simulated. No real card networks, PayPal accounts, or banks are contacted. Card numbers entered into the demo form are not transmitted to any processor and should not be real card numbers.

4. Storage & security

Data is stored in a SQLite database (development) or PostgreSQL (production) accessed only by the application server. Access tokens are short-lived (15 minutes); refresh tokens are stored in an HTTP-only cookie. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (12 rounds).

5. Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing privacy@yobou.market. Vendor records are retained for tax/audit purposes; order records may be retained in anonymized form.

6. Children's privacy

Yobou is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be announced in-app.

8. Contact

Questions: privacy@yobou.market
